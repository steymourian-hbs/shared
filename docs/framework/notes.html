Coding Convention

Page Headers - stick with h1 h2 p div tags

Page Content - Within Page Text, stick with h3 h4 p tags. Sidebar elements should start with h3

Dates

  1. single date, DD MON YYYY (01 NOV 2012)
  2. multiple dates, same month = DD-DD MON YYYY (12-13 DEC 2013)
  3. multiple dates, across months, same year = DD MON - DD MON YYYY (30 NOV - 01 DEC 2012)
  4. multiple dates, across years = DD MON YYYY - DD MON YYYY (31 DEC 2012 - 02 JAN 2013)
  5. multiple dates, skipped days DD MON YYYY, DD MON YYYY, and DD MON YYYY (31 DEC 2012, 02 JAN 2013, and 10 JAN 2013)

Wide hero Area

Page head and subhead/headline spans the first 11 columns

Blurb spans the first 10 columns

Example: Landing Template D.

General heading spacing standards

Space between white head and black subhead is 4 vertical grid units (32px) See MBA post-launch issue about subhead spacing.

Space from bottom of color band to text at bottom of color band is 5 vertical units (40px). See WWR #1371.

Space from bottom of color band to text at top of white content area is 5 vertical units (40px). See WWR #1371.

Space between primary color field and subnav is 32px.

Exception: "Landing Template C" (Eg. About Us Dean Landing) Distances are 40, 32, 56 from the color field to the tops of each respective column.

Top of vertical rule in primary color field should align with the baseline of the page title.

A vertical rule that does not bleed into the primary color field should begin 32 px below the bottom of the color field.

Footer

There should always be at least 40 pixels of white space above the closed footer.

Feed Event/Twitter Items

For feed/event/twitter items that have a date, two spaces should come after it before any new text follows.

For the item in the feed/event/twitter item that comes after the two spaces, it should be the color ash.
